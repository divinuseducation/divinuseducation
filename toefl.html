Test of English as a Foreign Language (TOEFL) is another International English Test used to test the ability of non-native speakers in English Communication.

TOEFL assesses your abilities in all four skills:

  • Listening
  • Reading
  • Writing
  • Speaking

All four tests have to be passed.

Internet based test format:

Task Description Approximate Time
Reading 3-5 passages,each containing 12-14 questions 60-80 minutes
Listening 6-9 passages, each containing 5-6 questions 60-90 minutes
Break Mandatory break 10 minutes
Speaking 6 tasks 20 minutes
Writing 2 tasks 50 minutes

Paper based test format:

Task Description Approximate Time
Reading 50 questions on passage 55 minutes
Listening 3 parts, The first one contains 30 questions about short conversations. The second part has 8 questions about longer conversations. The last part asks 12 questions about lectures or talks. 30-40 minutes
Structure and Written Expression 15 exercises of completing sentences correctly and 25 exercises of identifying errors 25 minutes
Writing 1 essay question 30 minutes
