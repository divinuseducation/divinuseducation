About Divinus Education

Divinus Education was founded in 2017 by Jenna and Deepa Patel, two native English speaking professionals with over 20 years worth of experience in English communication abroad. Since they were born and educated in the UK, they believe they can bring their expertise and professionalism to India and provide an insight into the culture and vibrant environment.


Jenna has previously worked as an accountant for a local government based in London, UK. She completed a Bachelors of Science in Mathematics and is a certified ACCA member (Association of Chartered Certified Accountants in England and Wales), working at the highest professional and ethical standards.


Deepa is an ambitious professional, having completed a Bachelors of Science in Mathematics with Finance and Economics from City, University of London. She also has a wealth of experience in helping those in learning English.


Our teachers are truly committed in not only providing you the support you need in learning and developing your understanding of English, but building an atmosphere of both living the English culture and language here in India!
