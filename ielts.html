IELTS

The International English Language Testing System (IELTS) is an International English Test used to test the ability of non-native speakers in English Communication.

There are two modules for IELTS:

  • IELTS General (For those who wish to undertake non-academic training for work purposes or immigration purposes)
  • IELTS Academic (For those who want to apply to universities abroad)

The IELTS test assesses your abilities in all four skills:

  • Listening
  • Reading
  • Writing
  • Speaking

All four tests have to be passed.

The listening and speaking tests are the same for all candidates, but the reading and writing differ for the academic or general version of the test.

Test Format:

Task Description Approximate Time
Reading 3 sections 30 minutes
Listening 4 sections, each containing 10 questions 30 minutes
Speaking 3 sections 11-14 minutes
Writing 2 tasks 60 minutes
